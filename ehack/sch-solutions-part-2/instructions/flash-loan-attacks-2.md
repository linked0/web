# Flash Loan Attacks Exercise 2

There is an Advanced Vault that allows depositing and withdrawing ETH.
This Vault also uses the deposited ETH liquidity to offer Flash Loans for users.
What could go wrong?

## Accounts

* 1 - Attacker (You)
