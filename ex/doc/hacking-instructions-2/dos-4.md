# Denial Of Service Exercise 4

## Intro
The Galactic Gorillas Yacht Club was founded by a group of elite space travelers in a galaxy far, far away.

You can go on interstellar safaris and intergalactic parties on the club's flagship yacht when you join. 

The minting event starts next week. The price is 1 ETH.

You were asked to audit their NFT smart contract and make sure it's secure before launch.
Can you break it?

(You start with 2.5 ETH in balance)

## Accounts
* 0 - Deployer & Owner
* 1 - User
* 2 - Attacker (You)

## Tasks

### Task 1
You've got to find a vulnerability that will break the contract and stop people from minting NFTs
